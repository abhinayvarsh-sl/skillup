Model Comparison

Strategic analysis of B2B Lead Gen vs B2C Monetisation.

Recommended

1. Flat (No Teams)

Goal 1: B2B Lead Gen
  • Zero Distraction: 100% focus on individual sign-ups (volume play).
  • Cleaner data (1 user = 1 human).
  • The "Hidden" Manager: Impossible to distinguish a CTO from a student.
  • Zero B2B signaling (no "invite team" button).
Goal 2: B2C Monetisation
  • Maximum Simplicity: Highest conversion rate for solo users. No "analysis paralysis".
  • Capped LTV: No built-in path to expand wallet share via team invites.

2. Hierarchical

Goal 1: B2B Lead Gen
  • Strongest Signal: "Start Team" identifies managers.
  • Sets up a team workspace instantly.
  • Visibility Risk: Users might miss the "Business" tab.
Goal 2: B2C Monetisation
  • Better Sales: Sell career growth to individuals, team tools to managers.
  • Can charge higher prices for specific value.
  • Small risk of users choosing the wrong path.

3. Category Based

Goal 1: B2B Lead Gen
  • Startup Friendly: Easier to upsell Pro plans to smaller companies.
  • Simple hook: "Want more topics?"
  • Revenue Cannibalization: Low-cost Pro plans eat into Enterprise deals.
  • Weak signal: Wanting more topics doesn't mean they manage a team.
Goal 2: B2C Monetisation
  • Easy Upgrade: Simpler path to upsell Pro plans.
  • Good for learners who want variety.
  • Specialists won't pay for extra topics.
  • High churn risk after finishing one topic.
